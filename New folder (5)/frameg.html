Another narrow-minded intention is seeking equality in an unfair world. Globalization attempted to make reseources available to everyone. In the Philippines, many find it difficult to buy food everyday yet most of them own a phone. The momentary pleasures help them ignore the harsh realities. Whatever intention globalization is given, it results in benefiting one social architype while taking form another.
